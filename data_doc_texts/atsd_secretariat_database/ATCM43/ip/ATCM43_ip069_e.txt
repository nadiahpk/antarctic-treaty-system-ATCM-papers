Turkey’s Full Membership to the SCAR
Information Paper submitted by Turkey
Summary
This paper presents information on the full membership process of
Turkey to the Scientific Committee on Antarctic Research (SCAR).
Background
To be involved in the initiation, development and coordination
processes of international scientific research in the Antarctic region,
Turkey applied for associated membership to the SCAR in 2016.
The associated membership application to SCAR was accepted
during the 34th Antarctic Scientific Research Committee Delegate
Meeting held in Kuala Lumpur, the capital of Malaysia, on 29-30
August 2016.
SCAR Full Membership
After 5 years of Antarctic expeditions and having considerable
scientific experience in polar research, Turkey applied for full
membership to SCAR in 2020. The application was evaluated by the
delegates during the 36th SCAR Delegates Meeting which was held
online on 22-25 March 2021. After the presentation of the Director
of the Scientific and Technological Research Council of Turkey
(TUBITAK) Marmara Research Center (MAM) Polar Research
Institute (PRI) the full membership request of Turkey was approved
by the voting of the delegates.
Conclusion
Turkey considers that this membership will enhance and give shape
to future polar research studies, and expresses its gratitude to all
the countries and their representatives who supported it throughout
the process.
