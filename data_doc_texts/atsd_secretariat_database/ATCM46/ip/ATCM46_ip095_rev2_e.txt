Report of the Depositary Government of the Antarctic Treaty and its
Protocol in accordance with Recommendation XIII-2
Summary
This report covers events with respect to the Antarctic Treaty and
the Protocol on Environmental Protection to the Antarctic Treaty.
Since the last report, there has been one new accession to the
Antarctic Treaty. The Kingdom of Saudi Arabia deposited its
instrument of accession on May 22, 2024. The Antarctic Treaty
entered into force for the Kingdom of Saudi Arabia on the date of
deposit of its instrument of accession. There have been no actions
with respect to the Protocol on Environmental Protection to the
Antarctic Treaty since the last report.
With respect to Measure 1 (2005) recommending that Annex VI on
Liability Arising from Environmental Emergencies form part of the
Environmental Protocol, the Czech Republic approved Measure 1
(2005) on May 21, 2024. With respect to Measure 16 (2009)
(Amended Annex II), the Czech Republic approved Measure 16
(2009) on May 21, 2024.
As of the date of this paper, there are fifty-seven (57) Parties to the
Treaty and forty-two (42) Parties to the Protocol.
