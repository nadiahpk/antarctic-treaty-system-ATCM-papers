Committee for Environmental Protection (CEP): summary of
activities during the 2023/24 intersessional period
Information Paper submitted by the acting CEP Chair
Summary
The 25th meeting of the Committee for Environmental Protection
(CEP XXIV) was held in a Helsinki, Finland from 28th May to 1st
June 2023. This paper presents a summary of deliveries to CEP 26
against the intersessional activities arising from CEP XXV. Where
appropriate, the details of related papers submitted to CEP 26 are
provided.
