Updated United States Policy on the Antarctic Region
Updated United States Policy on the Antarctic Region
Information Paper Submitted by the United States
Summary
On May 17, 2024, President Joseph R. Biden, Jr. signed a National
Security Memorandum on United States Policy on the Antarctic
Region. Under this policy, the United States will continue its
cooperative international efforts through the Antarctic Treaty
System to maintain the Antarctic Region for peaceful purposes,
protect its environment and ecosystems, and conduct scientific
research, long into the future.
The new policy is available at this link, as is an associated Fact
Sheet. Both are included as attachments.
