Committee for Environmental Protection (CEP): summary of

Information Paper submitted by the CEP Chair
Summary
The 23rd meeting of the Committee for Environmental Protection
(CEP XXIII) was held virtually in Paris, France, from 14 to 18 June
2021. This paper presents a summary of deliveries to CEP XXIV
against the intersessional activities arising from CEP XXIII, as
identified in CEP Circular: 1 / CEP XXIII. Where appropriate, the
details of related papers submitted to CEP XXIV are provided.
