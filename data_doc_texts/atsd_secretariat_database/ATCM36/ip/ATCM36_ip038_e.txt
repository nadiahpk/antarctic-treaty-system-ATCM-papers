Report of the Joint Inspections undertaken by the United Kingdom, the Netherlands and Spain under Article VII of the Antarctic Treaty and Article 14 of the Environmental Protocol


Information Paper submitted by the United Kingdom, the Netherlands and Spain 



ATCM XXXVI/WP009 presents the general recommendations arising from the Antarctic Treaty Inspections, which were conducted jointly by the United Kingdom, the Netherlands and Spain in the Antarctic Peninsula region, during December 2012.  The full Report from this Inspection Programme is now appended to this Information Paper (and hard copies will be distributed to Heads of Delegation at ATCM XXXVI).
