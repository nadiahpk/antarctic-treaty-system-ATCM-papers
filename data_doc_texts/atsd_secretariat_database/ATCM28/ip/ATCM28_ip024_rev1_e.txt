This report covers events with respect to the Antarctic Treaty and the Protocol on Environmental Protection.

There have been no new accessions to the Antarctic Treaty in the past year.  There are forty-five Parties to the Treaty.

The Czech Republic deposited its instrument of ratification of the Protocol on Environmental Protection on August 24, 2004.  There are now thirty-two Parties to the Protocol.
