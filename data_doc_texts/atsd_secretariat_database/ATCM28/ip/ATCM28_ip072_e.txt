Initial Environmental Evaluation for the establishment of a satellite reception and command facility (TrollSat) and a Norwegian Institute for Air Research (NILU) Laboratory as an integral part of the Troll Station in Dronning Maud Land, Antarctica


Information Paper
Submitted by Norway


The attached IEE-document, prepared by Kongsberg Satellite Services for the establishment of a satellite reception and command facility (TrollSat) and a NILU Laboratory as an integral part of the Troll Station in Dronning Maud Land, has been received by Norwegian authorities for consideration in accordance with the Regulations Pertaining to the Protection of the Environment in Antarctica (§§ 9 and 10). The document is presented to the CEP for information in conjunction with a separate working paper on the assessment of environmental impacts of satellite facility at Troll (cf. WP 40).
