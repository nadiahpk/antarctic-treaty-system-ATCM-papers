DECISION 1 (2004)
Revised Rules of Procedure (2004)

The Representatives,
Desiring to amend the Rules of Procedure for Antarctic Treaty Consultative Meetings;
Decide:
That the  “Revised Rules of Procedure (2004)” attached to this Decision shall replace the existing Rules of :Procedure for Antarctic Treaty Consultative Meetings with immediate effect.
