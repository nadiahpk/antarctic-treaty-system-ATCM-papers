DRAFT RESOLUTION XXX (2005)
SCIENTIFIC ISSUES RELATING TO THE IMPLEMENTATION OF MEASURES OF REINSTATEMENT OF THE ENVIRONMENT


The Representatives,

Recalling the undertaking in Article 16 of the Protocol on Environmental Protection,

Having regard to Decision […] (2005),

Invite the Scientific Committee on Antarctic Research, in collaboration with the Council of Managers of National Antarctic Programs, to submit, as soon as practicable, a working paper to the Antarctic Treaty Consultative Meeting on scientific issues relating to the implementation in the Antarctic Treaty area of measures aiming to reinstate or restore damaged or destroyed components of the environment, or to introduce the equivalent of these components into the environment.
