Measure XXX (2005)
Specially Protected Areas – Extension of Expiry Dates

The Representatives,

Recalling Recommendations VIII-4, XIII-8, XIV-5, XV-7 and XVI-2,

Noting that the expiry date for these sites is 31 December 2005, but wishing to continue to protect these sites until such time that their respective Management Plans have been revised in accordance with Annex V of the Environmental Protocol,

Recommend to their Governments the following Measure for approval in accordance with paragraph 4 of Article IX of the Antarctic Treaty:

that the date of expiry of the management plans in the list annexed to this Measure be extended until 31 December 2010, and that this Measure be applied provisionally, to the fullest extent possible consistent with their Government’s domestic laws and regulations, pending such approval.


Annex to Measure XXX (2005)

SPA 125 	Fildes Peninsula, King George Island, South Shetland Islands
SPA 127	Haswell Island
SPA 144	Chile Bay (Discovery Bay), Greenwich Island
