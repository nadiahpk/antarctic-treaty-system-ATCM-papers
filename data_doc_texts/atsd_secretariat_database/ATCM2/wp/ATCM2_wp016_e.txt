ITEM 16 OF THE AGENDA

PLACE AND DATE OF THE NEXT MEETING

Presented by the Delegation of Belgium

The Representatives recomend that their Governments accept the
offer made by the Delegation of Belgium, to the effect that the Third
Antarctic Consultative Meeting be held in Brussels, in accordance
with the provisions of Article IX of the Antarctic Treaty.

This Meeting will be held on a date to be decided upon by agree-
ment between the participating Governments.
