ITEM 17 OF THE AGENDA

OTHER MATTERS
Presented by New Zealand

The Representatives recommend to their Governments:

1.      That in future intersession meetings be convened at least
        quarterly by the Government which is to act as host for the next
        Consultative Meeting;

2.      that these meetings be held under the auspices of the
        diplomatic mission of that host Government in the capital of
        one of the Contracting Powers;

3.      that the capital should be selected by agreement among
        the Treaty Powers and should be one in which all the Treaty
        Powers are represented;

4.      that each host Government should be free in accordance
        with accepted practice to hold such other meetings as may be
        considered necessary to prepare for Consultative Meetings.

CONFIDENTIAL
