ITEM 6 OF THE AGENDA

MEASURES TO ENSURE THE EXCHANGE OF THE RESULTS AND DATA OF

SCIENTIFIC OBSERVATIONS CARRIED OUT IN ANTARCTICA

Presented by the Union of Socialist Soviet Republics

The Representatives recomend that their respective
governments adopt the necessary measures to facilitate:

1) The termination by July 1, 1963, for presentation to
the World Information Centres, of all data resulting from observa-
tions and scientific research carried out in Antarctica between
1955 and 1960.

2) The presentation to the World Information Centres of
all data resulting from observations and scientific research of all
kinds, within a term not in excess of one year from the date of the
return of the expedition which accomplished the observations and
investigations in the Antarctic.

3) The delivery to World Information Centres within two
months of their appearance of all publications containing the results
of Antarctic research. Publications appearing before July 31, 1962,
shall be delivered by July 1, 1963, at latest.
