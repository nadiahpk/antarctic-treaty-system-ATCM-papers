ITEM 13 OF AGENDA

INTERNATIONAL QUIET SUN YEAR (1964)

Presented by Australia

It is suggested that the following draft resolution
requires no explanatory memorandum:

The representatives recomend to their Governments
that they should encourage, by whatever means they consider appro-
priate, international cooperation and the interchange of scientific
personnel, observation and results, in connection with their res-
pective national programes of scientific investigation and research
associated with the International Quiet Sun Year (1964).
