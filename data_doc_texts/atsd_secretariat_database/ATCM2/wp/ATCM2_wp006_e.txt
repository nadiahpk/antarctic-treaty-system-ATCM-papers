ITEM 9 OF THE AGENDA

EXCHANGE OF INFORMATION UNDER ARTICLE VII.5

Presented by Australia

Recalling the provisions of Article VII.5 of the Antarctic
Treaty, the representatives recomend to their governments that
they should endeavour to furnish prompt and full information
regarding their Antarctic activities in accordance with the
agreed list set out in Recomendation VI of the First Consultative
Meeting, and within the time limits indicated in that recommenda-
tion.
