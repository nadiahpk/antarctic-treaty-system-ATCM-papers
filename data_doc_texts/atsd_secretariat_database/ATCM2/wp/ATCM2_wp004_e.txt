POINT 7 OF THE AGENDA

MEASURES FOR THE PROTECTION AND CONSERVATION OF LIVING
RESOURCES AND EXCHANGE OF INFORMATION ON THE SUBJECT

(Presented by the Chilean Delegation)

"The Representatives recommend that their respective Governments:

1.      Compile information on live resources, their increasing
        extinction in the antarctic and the measures to be taken
        for their protection and conservation.

2.      Establish a committee of experts to study these data and
        draw up a project of agreement, which should be submitted
        to the Governments for their consideration, so that a
        final decision thereon may be reached at the next Consul-
        tative Meeting.

3.      That pending this agreement, general policies of conduct
        be adopted, similar to those recommended by "SCAR" on this
        subject, referred to in Recommendation I-VII of the Can-
        berra Meeting.

4.      That such policies be effectively applied by the heads
