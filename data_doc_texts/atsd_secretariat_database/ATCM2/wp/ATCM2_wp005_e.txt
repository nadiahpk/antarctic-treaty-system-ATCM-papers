POINT 9 OF THE AGENDA

COMPLEMENTATION OF THE EXCHANGE OF INFORMATION SUPPLIED IN
ACCORDANCE WITH ARTICLE VII, PARAGRAPH 5 OF THE ANTARCTIC
TREATY AND RECOMMENDATION I-VI OF THE FIRST CONSULTATIVE
MEETING.

(Presented by the Argentine Delegation)

The representatives recomend to their respective
Governments that, as soon as possible, and in any case prior to
June 30 of each year, and through diplomatic channels, they
report on the results of the activities previously reported in
accordance with Article VII, Paragraph 5 of the Treaty, and
Recommendation I-VI of the First Consultative Meeting.

In case the aforementioned and already reported
activities should have been extended, reduced or modified,
additional information should be furnished at this time.

POINT 11 OF THE AGENDA

EXCHANGE OF INFORMATION REGARDING THE CARRYING
OUT OF THE PROPOSED ANTARCTIC ACTIVITIES

Presented by the Secretariat

The following draft is the result of a working group debate
held on July 20, in connection with the draft recommendation pre-
sented by Argentina (P.5):

The representatives recomend to their respective Governments
that they report through diplomatic channels, as soon as possible,
and in any case prior to June 30 of each year, on any extensions,
reductions or modifications in the development of the activities
previously reported in accordance with Article VII, Paragraph 5 of
the Treaty, and Recommendation I-VI of the First Consultative Meeting.
