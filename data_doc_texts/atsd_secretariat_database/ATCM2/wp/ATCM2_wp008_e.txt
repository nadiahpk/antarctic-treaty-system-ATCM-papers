POINT 12 OF THE AGENDA

ADMINISTRATIVE FACILITIES FOR INCOMING AND OUTGOING SAMPLES
SPECIMENS AND SCIENTIFIC INSTRUMENTS

Presented by Argentina

The Representatives recommend to their Governments,
in view of the provisions of articles II and III of the Antarctic
Treaty and of the recomendations I-I and I-II of the First Consul
tative Meeting and in order to contribute to their better implemen
tation;

a)  that they provide special facilities in the proceedings for
receiving or dispatching samples and scientific speciens
(biological, geological, geophysical, bacteriological)
celluloid, paper or phonomagnetic tapes recording scientific
data, and scientific instruments which in view of Antarctic
international scientific cooperation, may be sent to or emanate
from, either singly or accompanied, another signatory nation
of the Antarctic Treaty;

b)  that they bear in mind in this respect, the need for some of
these specimens, samples, tapes and instruments to be trans-
ported so as to comply with specific special requirements,
and at times within brief intervals, in order to be able to
fulfil the object for which they are despatched from one
country to another.

POINT 12 OF THE AGENDA

ADMINISTRATIVE FACILITIES FOR INCOMING AND OUTGOING SAMPLES
SPECIMENS AND SCIENTIFIC INSTRUMENTS

Presented by the Secretariat

The Representatives recommend to their Governments
that, in order better to implement the provisions of articles II
and III of the Antarctic Treaty and of recommendations I-I and I-II
of the First Consultative Meeting, they should make appropriate
arrangements;

a)  to expedite the application of their laws and regulations and
    international agreements governing shipments of samples, speci-
    mens, records and scientific instruments related to Antarctic
    scientific research;

b)  to provide proper care in the handling of this type of
    shipment.
