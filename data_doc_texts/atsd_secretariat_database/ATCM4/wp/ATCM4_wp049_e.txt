PROTECTED AREAS TO BE LISTED IN ANNEX B     
OF THE AGREED MEASURES FOR THE CONSERVA-    
TION OF ANTARCTIC FAUNA AND FLORA           

United Kingdom Working Paper               

10 November 1966                           

The Specially Protected Areas to be listed in Annex B of
the Agreed Measures for the Conservation of Antarctic Fauna
and Flora are listed below in numerical order, starting at
the Greenwich Meridian (0o) and moving eastwards.  Numbers
4 and 5 have been left blank in order that two areas to be
proposed by the French Delegation may be inserted in their
proper position.
