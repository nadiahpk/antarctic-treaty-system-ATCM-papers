SPECIALLY PROTECTED SPECIES: FUR SEALS

The Representatives recommend to their Governments that
the following be inserted in Annex A, Specially Protected Spe-
cies, of the Agreed Measures for the Conservation of Antarctic
Fauna and Flora:

"All species of the genus Arctocephalus, Fur Seals".
