REVISED DRAFT RECOMMENDATION SUBMITTED BY U.S.A.

Exchange of Information on Operations

The Representatives recommend to their Governments that
they exchange through diplomatic channels not later than 31st
October of each year, the information specified in as many as
possible of the categories in Recommendations I - VI, III - I
and III - II, the balance to be furnished as soon as possible
thereafter and in any case before the end of November.
