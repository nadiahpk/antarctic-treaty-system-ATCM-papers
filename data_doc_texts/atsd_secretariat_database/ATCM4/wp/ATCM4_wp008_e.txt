A R G E N T I N E                      


SYMPOSIUM ON LOGISTICS AND POLAR TECHNIQUE


The Sympoium's agenda could comprise the follow-
ing group of topics:


1.-    Relations between science and polar technique;


2.-    Permanent Antarctic construction (Dwellings, observatories,
       laboratories, airfields, power plants, reservoirs, engineer-
       ing structures for scientific purposes, etc.);


3.-    Land, sea and air transportation media;


4.-    Field clothing and equipment;


5.-    Safety measures at the bases and in the field airport and
       assistance.


6.-    Measures connected with food, health and welfare;


7.-    Communications;


8.-    Supplies and their replacement;


9.-    Land, sea and air operations;


10.-   Organization and general administration;


11.-   Installations for observation of satellites.
