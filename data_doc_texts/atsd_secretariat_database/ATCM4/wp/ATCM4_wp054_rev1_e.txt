SPECIALLY PROTECTED AREAS: CAPE HALLETT, VICTORIA LAND

The Representatives, considering that Cape Hallett inclu-
des a small patch of particularly rich and diverse vegetation
which supports a variety of terrestrial fauna and that the eco-
system, which includes a rich avian fauna, is of outstanding scien-
tific interest, recommend to their Governments that the following
be inserted in Annex B, Specially Protected Areas, of the Agreed
Measures for the Conservation of Antarctic Fauna and Flora:-

SPECIALLY PROTECTED AREA No 7

CAPE HALLETT, VICTORIA LAND

Lat. 72°18'S., Long. 170°19'E.

DESCRIPTION:  The Area between the eastern side of the road, which
runs along the eastern side of Willett Cove, and the western margin
of the permanent ice sheet, to the south of a line from the road to
the margin of the permanent ice sheet at the latitude of the head of
Willett Cove, and to the north of a line from the road to the mar-
gin of the permanent ice sheet drawn 350 meters to the south of
that latitude and parallel to it.  The Area is shown on the attached
map.
