DATE OF EXCHANGE OF INFORMATION

The Representatives recommend to their Governments that they exchange through diplomatic channels not later than october 31st of each year, the information specified in as many as possible of the categories in Recommendations I-VI, III-I and III-II, the balance to be furnished as soon as possible thereafter and in any case before the end of November.
