DRAFT RECOMMENDATION PRESENTED BY CHILE

Amendment to Recommendation III-VIII

The Representatives recommend to their Governments that 
paragraph (d) of Article II of Recommendation III-VIII adopted 
by the Third Consultative Meeting be amended to read as fol-
lows :

"(d) "Appropriate authority" means any captain of ship or 
aircraft,  expedition leader, or head of an Antarctic base 
authorized by a Participating Government to issue permits under 
these Agreed Measures".
