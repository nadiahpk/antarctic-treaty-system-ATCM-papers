SPECIALLY PROTECTED SPECIES: ROSS SEAL

The Representatives recommend to their Governments that
the following species be inserted in Annex A, Specially Pro-
tected Species, of the Agreed Measures for the Conservation of
Antarctic Fauna and Flora:

"Ommatophoca rossi, Ross Seal".
