STATUS OF MEETINGS OF EXPERTS

(United Kingdom proposed re-draft of paragraphs 1 and 2 of United
States Working Paper circulated on 6 September, 1966)

1.-  That Meetings of Experts be convened from time to time as
the need arises to discuss practical problems of programme
activity in Antarctica. Such Meetings may be attended by
experts from Consultative Parties and by any independent
experts invited to attend by all the Consultative Parties.

2.-  That such Meetings be convened and the Agenda and specific
objectives be established by agreement between all those
Parties entitled to participate in Consultative Meetings
under the Antarctic Treaty who wish to participate in the
Meetings of Experts.
