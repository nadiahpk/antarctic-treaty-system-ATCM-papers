DRAFT RECOMMENDATION SUBMITTED BY AUSTRALIA

Exchange of Information on Operations

The Representatives recommend to their Governments that 
they transmit, in time to reach other Governments not later
than 31st October of each year, the information requested in 
as many as possible of the categories listed in Recommenda-
tions I - VI, II - I and III - II, the balance to be furnish-
ed as soon as possible thereafter and in any case before the 
end of November.
