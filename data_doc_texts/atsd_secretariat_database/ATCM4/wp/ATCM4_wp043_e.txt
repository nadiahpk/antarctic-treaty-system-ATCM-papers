DRAFT RECOMMENDATION PRESENTED BY CHILE

ITEM 10 OF THE  AGENDA

9 NOVEMBER 1966

The Representatives recommend to their Governments that:

a) They accept the Recommendations contained in Document
IX-SCAR-39, on the pelagic hunting of seals and the
taking of fauna on pack ice;

b) They urge SCAR to proceed as soon as possible with the
establishment of the Sub-Committee referred to in paragraph
1 of Document IX-SCAR-39, which is to prepare a scientific
report for consideration by the Fifth Antarctic Treaty
Consultative Meeting.
