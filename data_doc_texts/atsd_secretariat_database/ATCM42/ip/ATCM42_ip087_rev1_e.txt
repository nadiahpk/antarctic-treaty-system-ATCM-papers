Future Antarctic Science Challenges
Compilation of input from Parties to informal intersessional discussions
Information Paper submitted by Australia, Finland, India, Spain, Turkey and United Kingdom 
Summary
This paper provides a compilation of information provided by Parties in discussions of Future Antarctic Science Challenges and shared science priorities. Working Paper 32 reports on the outcomes of these discussions. The broad objectives of the discussions were to discuss Parties Strategic national science plans/priorities, identify areas of mutual scientific interest and consider collaborative opportunities. Participants provided responses to guiding questions on science priorities, and a compilation of these responses is attached to this paper. The intersessional discussions also considered relevant papers submitted by Parties to the ATCM. The attached includes a table that briefly summarises the research objectives identified in those papers.
