Information Paper submitted by Belarus and Turkey

Summary 
This paper gives information about the signing of a Memorandum of Understanding between the Republic of Turkey and Belarus within the context of international cooperation in scientific research. 
Memorandum of Understanding Between Republic of Turkey and Belarus

The main objective of the activity of the Republic of Turkey in Antarctica at the present stage and in the long term is obtaining new knowledge, development and deepening of the international cooperation for the benefit of science and progress of all mankind. 

According to provisions of Articles 2 and 3 of the Antarctic Treaty the Republic of Turkey seeks to develop international cooperation in scientific research in Antarctica. Within realization by the Turkish National Polar Scientific Program (2018-2022) Turkey has a big readiness to develop the relations of scientific cooperation with all parties of the Antarctic Treaty.

In this context a Memorandum of Understanding between The Ministry of Industry and Technology of the Republic of Turkey and the National Academy of Sciences of Belarus on the Scientific and Technical Cooperation in Antarctica has been signed on 16 April 2019. The Memorandum of Understanding mainly includes Cooperation Method and Priorities of Scientific Cooperation.

The Republic of Turkey would like to thank the Belarussian Government for this opportunity. Such cooperation is extremely important for both parties in their aspiration to take an active part in the further development of the system of the Antarctic Treaty and to strengthen international cooperation.
