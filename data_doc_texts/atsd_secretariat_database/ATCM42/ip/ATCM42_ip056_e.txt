The Harmonization of Turkish Law to the Protocol on Environmental Protection to the Antarctic Treaty
Summary
This paper gives a brief report on Turkey’s studies for the harmonization of Turkish Law to the Protocol on Environmental Protection to the Antarctic Treaty and the way forward.
Introduction

The law on ratification of the Protocol on Environmental Protection to the Antarctic Treaty and all of its Annexes has been published in 24.05.2017 dated and 30075 numbered Official Gazette. Following this, standard procedures have been employed and the Depositary Government has been notified. 

As stated in the Circular 30/2017 published by Secretariat on 07/12/2017, Depositary Government has informed Secretariat that Turkey has acceded to the Protocol on Environmental Protection to the Antarctic Treaty. The official date of the deposit of the instrument of accession was 27 September 2017.

In addition to this information, Turkey has been working on harmonization its domestic law to the Protocol on Environmental Protection to the Antarctic Treaty. The first draft is about to be circulated among stakeholder institutions. 

The current Environmental Impact Assessment legislation is not suitable to be implemented for Antarctic activities. Resolution 1 (2016) Annex: Guidelines for Environmental Impact Assessment in Antarctica is considered and annexed to draft legislation.
