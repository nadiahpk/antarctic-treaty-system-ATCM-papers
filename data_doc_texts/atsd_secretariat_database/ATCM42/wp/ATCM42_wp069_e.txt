Intention of Ukraine to accede to the Convention for the Conservation of Antarctic Seals
Working Paper submitted by Ukraine


Ukraine as a Consultative Party to the Antarctic Treaty expresses its intention to accede to the Convention for the Conservation of Antarctic Seals (CCAS) and thereby makes its contribution to further strengthen the Antarctic Treaty system. At the moment, the government negotiations are conducted within the domestic procedure of acceding to the CCAS in accordance with the legislation of Ukraine. 
In accordance with the provisions of Article 12 of the Convention, Ukraine would like to request the United Kingdom as a Depositary Government for the Convention to initiate a procedure for receiving notifications of the consent of all Contracting Parties to invite Ukraine to accede to the CCAS.
