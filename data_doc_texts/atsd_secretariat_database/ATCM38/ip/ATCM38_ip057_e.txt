Report of the Joint Inspections undertaken by the United Kingdom and the Czech Republic under Article VII of the Antarctic Treaty and Article 14 of the Environmental Protocol

Information Paper submitted by the United Kingdom and the Czech Republic


ATCM XXXVIII/WP019 presents the general recommendations arising from the Antarctic Treaty Inspections, which were conducted jointly by the United Kingdom and the Czech Republic in the Antarctic Peninsula region from December 2014 to January 2015.

The final text of this Inspection Programme report is appended to this Information Paper. Once ready, this paper will be updated to append an electronic version of the full published Report with finalised graphics. The text of the report will not change. The publication version is expected to be ready at least two weeks prior to the start of the ATCM. Hard copies will be distributed to Heads of Delegations at ATCM XXXVIII.
