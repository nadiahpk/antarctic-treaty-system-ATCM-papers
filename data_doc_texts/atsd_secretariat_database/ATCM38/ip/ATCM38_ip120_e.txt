Workshop on Education and Outreach:
Summary of CCAMLR initiatives

Abstract


A summary of the CCAMLR’s initiatives relating to capacity building, support to early career professionals, awareness raising and public relations is presented.  The initiatives, which supplement related activities supported by individual CCAMLR Members at the national level, include partnerships, internships, scholarships and awareness raising and public relations focussed on CCAMLR’s website and use of social media.
