Results of RACER workshop focused on James Ross Island

Information Paper presented by the United Kingdom and the Czech Republic


The attached report summarises a RACER (Rapid Assessment of Circum-Arctic Ecosystem Resilience) workshop which took place in November 2014 and identified key features within the James Ross Island area that could confer resilience to the wider North East Atlantic Peninsula ecoregion. This document provides further detail in relation to ATCM XXXVIII/WP038.
