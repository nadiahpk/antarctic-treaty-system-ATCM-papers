DISPOSAL OF NUCLEAR WASTE

(Draft Recommendation by Australia, Belgium and
New Zealand.)

The Representatives,

Recalling the undertaking of Contracting Parties in
Article X of the Treaty to exert appropriate efforts con-
sistent with the Charter of the United Nations to the end
that no one engages in any activity in Antarctica contrary
to the principles or purposes of the Treaty;

Bearing in mind that Article V of the Antarctic Treaty
is binding only on the Contracting Parties;

Desiring to preserve the unique quality of the Antarctic
environment;

Recommend to their Governments that;

They use their best endeavours to prevent the disposal
of nuclear waste in the Antarctic Treaty area.
