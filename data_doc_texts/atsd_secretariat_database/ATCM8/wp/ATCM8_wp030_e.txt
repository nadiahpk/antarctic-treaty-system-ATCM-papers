MARINE LIVING RESOURCES IN ANTARCTICA

Amendments proposed by Australia and New Zealand to ANT/16.

1.  Delete the fourth preambular paragraph and insert the
    following preambular paragraph in its place:

    "Recognizing that the prime need is to develop as a
    matter of urgency measures which will ensure the
    conservation and proper management of these living
    resources."

2.  Insert a new operative paragraph reading as follows:

    1.  They appoint a special group of experts to
        meet as soon as practicable to consider the
        development of measures to conserve and manage
        fisheries in the Treaty area.

3.  Recommendation 1 becomes Recommendation 2

4.  Recommendation 2 becomes Recommendation 3

5.  Recommendation 3 becomes Recommendation 4
