ITEM 6

OPERATION OF THE ANTARCTIC TREATY SYSTEM

IMPLEMENTATION OF RECOMMENDATION XIII.6, 2(b)

(Working paper submitted by the Australian Delegation)

Paper may be made publicly available at
the conclusion of the meeting
The Australian Delegation wishes to remind the Meeting of the need to implement paragraph 2 (b) of Recommendation 6 of the 12th Antarctic Treaty Consultative Meeting which reads as follows:

"as and when the Representative of the Consultative Parties consider it appropriate, draw the attention of any Specialised Agency of the United Nations or other international organisation having a scientific or technical interest in Antarctica to any part of the Report of the Consultative Meeting, or any information document submitted to the Meeting and made available to the public, relevant to the scientific or technical interest which that agency or organisation has in Antarctica;"
