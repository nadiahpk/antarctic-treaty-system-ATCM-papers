Recommendations of the Antarctic Treaty Meeting
on Telecommunications. (Washington 24-28 June 1963)

Recommendation proposed by Australia:

"The representatives, noting that an Antarctic
Treaty Meeting on Telecommunications was held in Washington
from 24th-28th June, 1963, following upon recommendations
Numbers I-XI and II-III of the First and Second Consultative
Meetings, recommend to their Governments that they take
appropriate steps to implement as soon as practicable those
recommendations of the Telecommunications Meeting which
are applicable to them".
