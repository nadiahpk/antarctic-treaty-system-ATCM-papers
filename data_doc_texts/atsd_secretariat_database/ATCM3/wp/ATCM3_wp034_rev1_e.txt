FAUNA AND FLORA

Australian Amendments to Doc.P/30 (Article IV)

Paragraph 2 (b) replace the words "within 200 metres of"
by the words "close to, for example, within about
200 metres of".

Paragraph 2 (e) replace the words "at a distance of less
than 300 metres" by the words "close to, for example,
within about 300 metres of".

Paragraph 2, last section. Amend to read as follows:

"However, the above activities, except those set
forth in (a) and (e) above, may be permitted to
the minimum extent necessary for the establishment,
supply and operation of stations."
