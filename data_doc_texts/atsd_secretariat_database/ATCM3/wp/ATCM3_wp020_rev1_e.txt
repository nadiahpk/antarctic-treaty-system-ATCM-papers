Exchange of Information on Aviation

Facilities

The following recommendation has been adopted:

The Representatives, taking into account Recommendation
I-VI (8) of the First Consultative Meeting, recommend to
their Governments that they exchange information, within
the framework of Recommendation I-VI (8), on airfield
facilities in Antarcitca, including information on location,
operating conditions and limitations, available radio aids
to navigation, radio communications facilities, and instrument
landing facilities, and in sufficient detail that an aviator
may make a safe landing when necesiary.
