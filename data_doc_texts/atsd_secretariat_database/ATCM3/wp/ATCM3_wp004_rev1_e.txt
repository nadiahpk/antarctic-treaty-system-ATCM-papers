ITEM 1 of the AGENDA                      

PELAGIC SEALING                           

POINT PROPOSED OR RECOMMENDATION BY      
THE DELEGATIONS OF AUSTRALIA, UNITED      
STATES OF AMERICA, NORWAY, UNITED         
KINGDOM AND USSR.                         

The Representatives, at the time of adopting  
the agreed measures on the Conservation of Fauna and Flora,
Considcring that appropriate voluntary regulation  
of pelagic sealing or the taking of fauna on pack ice is
of great importance for the fulfillment of the purposes  
and principles of these measures;                       

Recommend to their Governments that when ships of  
their nationality engage in pelagic sealing or the taking
of fauna on pack ice south of 60° South Latitude, each   
Government voluntarily regulate these activities to ensure
the survival of any species being taken and to ensure that 
the natural ecological system is not seriously disturbed.
