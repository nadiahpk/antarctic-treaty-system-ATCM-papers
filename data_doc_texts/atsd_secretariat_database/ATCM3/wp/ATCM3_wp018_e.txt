Item 6 on the Agenda

MEASURES TO FACILITATE THE EXERCISE OF INSPECTION RIGHTS
IN THE ANTARCTIC

Draft recommandation proposed by Chile.

"That the representatives, in consideration of the
"provisions of Article VII, paragraphs 1,2,3 and 4, and of Ar-
"ticle IX, paragraphs 1(d) and 3, of the Antarctic Treaty,
"recommend their Governments to exchange,through diplomatic
"channels,information on the results of inspections in the
"zone covered by the Treaty with the purpose of improving know-
"ledge about possible methods of exercising the rights of ins-
"pection.

" The representatives also recommend that, during the
"meetings preparatory to the Fourth Consultative Meeting of the
"Antarctic Treaty Powers, consultations take place on measures
"intended to facilitate the exercise of the inspection rights
"provided for in Article VII of the Treaty, in consideration of
"the experience acquired during the inspections already carried
"out".
