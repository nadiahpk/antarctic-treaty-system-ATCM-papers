CONSERVATION OF FAUNA AND FLORA
Revision of Article X on Amendments
proposed by the United States of America:

1.      The Annexes in particular may be amended as necessary
        through diplomatic channels.

2.      An amendment proposed through diplomatic correspondence
        shall be submitted in writing to the depositary Government
        (that is, the Government of Belgium), which shall communicate
        it to all of the Participating Governments for their
        approval.

3.      Any amendment shall become effective six months after
        the date on which notifications of approval have been
        received by the depositary Government from all of the
        Participating Governments.

4.      The depositary Government shall notify the Participating
        Governments of the date of receipt of each approval
        communicated to it and the date on which the amendment
        will become effective.
