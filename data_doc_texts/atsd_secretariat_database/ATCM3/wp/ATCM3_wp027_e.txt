DRAFT RECOMMENDATION PROPOSED BY THE
BELGIAN DELEGATION CONCERNING THE
IMPLEMENTATION OF THE AGREED MEASURES

Those participating Governments, whose expeditions
use ships sailing under flags of nationalities other than
their own, shall recommend these expeditions to take the
appropriate steps for ensuring that the crews of these
vessels observe the Agreed Measures.
