Agreed Measures for the Conservation of Wild Life in the Antarctic.

United Kingdom draft proposal for a Recommendation of the 
Third Consultative Meeting:

The Representatives, taking into consideration Article 
IX of the Antarctic Treaty, and recalling Recommendation I -
VIII of the First Consultative Meeting and Recommendation II -
II of the Second Consultative Meeting, recommend to their 
governments that they implement the annexed "Agreed Measures 
for the Conservation of Wild Life in the Antarctic" and that 
each, pending such implementation, should initiate as soon as 
possible appropriate administrative action in conformity with 
these agreed measures. They further recommend that the 
governments consult through the diplomatic channel with a 
view to agreement at the Fourth Consultative Meeting on the 
Schedules of "specially protected species" and "specially 
protected areas" provided for in these Agreed Measures
