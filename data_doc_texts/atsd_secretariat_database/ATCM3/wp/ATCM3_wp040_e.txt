AGENDA ITEM No.1

RECOMMENDATION BY GREAT BRITAIN
AND THE UNITED STATES OF AMERICA

The Representatives recommend to their Governments
that until such time as the Agreed Measures on
Conservation may become effective in accordance with
Article IX of the Antarctic Treaty, those Agreed Measures
as far as possible be considered as guide-lines in this
interim period.
