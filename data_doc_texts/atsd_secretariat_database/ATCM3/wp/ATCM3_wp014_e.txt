APPLICATION OF ARTICLE IX
PARAGRAPH 2 OF THE ANTARCTIC TREATY.

Australian Proposal

"Any new contracting Party whose Antarctic activity
entitles it to participate in the meetings held in
accordance with Article IX, 1 of the Treaty should be
encouraged to accept the measures approved by the other
contracting parties entitled to participate in meetings."
