ITEM 8 of the AGENDA

DRAFT RECOMMENDATION CONCERNING THE
MEETINGS OF EXPERTS

While recognizing the acuteness of the problem
of the meetings of experts, the representatives recommend
to their governments, to examine it carefully during the
period proceeding the IV  Consultative Meeting (especially
in the preparatory meetings), in order to reach a satisfactory
solution at the end of this  IV  Meeting.
