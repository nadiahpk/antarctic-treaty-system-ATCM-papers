AGENDA ITEM N° 1

NEW ARTICLE TO BE INSERTED IN THE AGREED
MEASURES

Those participating Governments, whose expeditions
use ships sailing under flags of nationalities other than
their own shall arrange as far as feasible with the owners
of such ships that the crews of these ships observe the
present agreed measures.
