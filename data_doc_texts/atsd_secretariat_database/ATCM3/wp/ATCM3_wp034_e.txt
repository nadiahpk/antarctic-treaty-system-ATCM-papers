AGENDA ITEM NO.1

FAUNA AND FLORA

Australian Amendments to Doc.P/30 (Article IV)

Paragraph 2(b) replace the words "within 200 metres of"
by the words "close to".

Paragraph 2(e) replace the words "at a distance of less
than 300 metres" by the words "close to".

Paragraph 2, last section, after the word "effective"
add the words "and in the case of establishment
of new stations".

Add a new sentence to this section reading as
follows: "It is recognised that establishment of
new stations might also involve some unavoidable
infringement of (f) above but this should be kept
to a minimum."
