ITEM 9 of the Agenda

APPLICATION OF ARTICLE IX
PARAGRAPH 2 OF THE ANTARCTIC TREATY.

(Submitted by Delegation of South Africa)

Any new Contracting Party whose Antarctic activity
entitles it to participate in the meetings held in accordance
with Article IX, 1 of the Treaty should be encouraged to
accept the measures app[?]roved by the other contracting parties
entitled to participate in meetings.  This encouragement
might entail making app[?]ropriate arrangements to inform the
new Contracting Party to advise whether it is able and
willing to accept and implement the Recommendations.
