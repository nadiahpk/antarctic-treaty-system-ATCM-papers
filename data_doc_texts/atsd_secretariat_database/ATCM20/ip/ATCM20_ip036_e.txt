(Agenda item 7)

(Submitted by Argentina & Australia)
INTERNATIONAL SYMPOSIUM ON THE FUTURE OF THE ANTARCTIC TREATY SYSTEM

Ushuaia, Argentina 20 to 24 March 1995

•
Argentina and Australia circulated a brief report about the International Symposium on the Future of the Antarctic Treaty System at ATCM XIX in 1995 (XIX ATCM/INF 54). The symposium, organised by the Consejo Argentino para las Relaciones Internacionales (CARI) and the Australian Antarctic Foundation, was held in Ushuaia, Argentina, from 20 to 24 March 1995.

Attached, for the information of Antarctic Treaty Consultative and Non-Consultative Parties, is the published proceedings of that symposium.
