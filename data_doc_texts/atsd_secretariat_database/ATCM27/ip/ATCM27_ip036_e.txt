REPORT OF THE DEPOSITARY GOVERNMENT
OF THE ANTARCTIC TREATY AND ITS PROTOCOL (USA)
IN ACCORDANCE WITH RECOMMENDATION XIII-2

This report covers events with respect to the Antarctic Treaty and the Protocol on Environmental Protection.

There have been no new accessions to the Antarctic Treaty in the past year.  There are forty-five Parties to the Treaty.

Canada deposited its instrument of ratification of the Protocol on Environmental Protection on November 13, 2003.  There are now thirty-one Parties to the Protocol.
