PREFACE


The Direction of Environment Affairs of the Ministry  of Foreign Affairs, through its Antarctic Department, prepared during year 2003, the “Chilean Compendium of the Antarctic Treaty System” which contains the main legal tools of said System, relevant national information about the Antarctica and a List of National Contact Points of the Antarctic System which is better accomplished compared to the one delivered at the end of each Consultative Meeting.

Said Compendium was distributed in December 2003 to the Foreign Affairs Ministry and corresponding Polar Institutes by the Chilean Embassies established in the countries that are components of the Contracting Parties of the Antarctic Treaty.

In order to contribute with the preparation of the Data Bank of the Treaty Secretary Office in Buenos Aires and in order to offer a useful information to the members of the Delegations participants in the XXVII Consultative Meeting of the Antarctic Treaty, the Chilean Delegation has allowed itself, through this Informative Document, to attach hereby the List of Contact Points more completed and updated.

The texts of the Compendium, the Chilean Legislation about the Antarctica and the abovementioned List, can be found in the following Web Page :

http://www.minrel.cl/pages/politicos/ambiente/antartica.html


We would highly appreciate to every member of the delegations that wished to update, modify, correct or suggest about any issue, to contact Mrs. María Luisa Carvallo, Chief of the Antarctic Department to email mlcarvallo@minrel.gov.cl
