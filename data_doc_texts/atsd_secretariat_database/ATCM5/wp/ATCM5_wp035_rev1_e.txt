DRAFT RECOMMENDATION ARISING
FROM ITEM 8 OF THE AGENDA
(Commemorative Stamp Issue)
tabled by the French Delegation

The Representatives forward the following recommendations
to their Governments :

1) that, in connection with the Tenth anniversary of the coming
into force of the Antarctic Treaty, each Member country
should issue a commemorative postage stamp during 1971 ;

2) that this stamp should bear, in the language or languages
of each issuing country, the words :

"The Antarctic Treaty, 1961-1971" ;

3) that the most prominent design feature of the stamp should
be constituted by the same representation of the Antarctic
Continent as appears on the official letter-headings of the
Antarctic Treaty Consultative Meetings ;

4) that any additional matter should be consonant with the
spirit of the Antarctic Treaty covenants ;

5) that the denomination of the stamp should remain at the
discretion of the issuing country.

RECOMMENDATION Nr. 1

(Commemorative Stamp Issue)

The Representatives recommend to their Governments :

1)  that, on the occasion of the Tenth anniversary of the entry
    into force of the Antarctic Treaty, each Consultative Party
    should issue a commemorative postage stamp during 1971;

2)  that this stamp should bear, in the language or languages
    of each issuing country, the following words :

    "Antarctic Treaty 1961 - 1971";

3)  that the most prominent feature of the stamp should be the
    Antarctic Treaty emblem representing a map of the Antarctica
    which appears on the official documents of Consultative
    Meetings;

4)  that any additional matter should be consonant with the
    provisions and the spirit of the Antarctic Treaty;

5)  that the denomination of the stamp should remain at the
    discretion of each issuing country.
