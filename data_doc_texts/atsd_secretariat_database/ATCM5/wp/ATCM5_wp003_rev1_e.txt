CHILEAN ADDITIONAL PROPOSAL
DRAFT AGENDA OF THE Vth. CONSULTATIVE MEETING
OF THE ANTARCTIC TREATY

Modifications to the agreed measures of Brussel's
Recommendation III-8 for preservations of living
resources in Antarctica

(a) Delete paragraph (d) of Article II as the wording of Article III
is considered satisfactory :

"Each Participating Government shall take appropriate
action to carry out these Agreed Measures."

(b) Amend the text of paragraph (e) of Article II as follows :

"(e) 'Permit' means a formal permission in writing".

(c) Amend the text of paragraph 6 of Article VI as follows :

"A participating Government shall not authorize the issue
of a permit with respect to Specially Protected Species
except in accordance with paragraph 7 of this Article".
