UNITED STATES PROPOSAL FOR HISTORIC SITES

To further implement Recommendation I-IX of the Antarctic Treaty the United States proposes the following items be added to a definitive list of "tombs, expeditionary artifacts and objects of historical interest".

1. Our Lady of the Snows Shrine erected on Hut Point Peninsula, 330 yards North-East of Scott's Hut (Lat. 77°51'S., Long. 166°37'E.) ;

2. Richard E. Byrd Memorial next to Chapel at McMurdo Station. (Lat. 77°51'S., Long. 166°37'E.).
