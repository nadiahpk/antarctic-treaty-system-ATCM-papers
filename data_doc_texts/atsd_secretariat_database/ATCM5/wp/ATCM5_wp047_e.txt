DRAFT RECOMMENDATION
CONCERNING THE NEXT ANTARCTIC TREATY
CONSULTATIVE MEETING
=========================

The Representatives recommend that their Governments
accept the invitation of the Japanese Government to hold
in Tokyo, during 1970, a Consultative Meeting under the
terms of Article IX of the Antarctic Treaty, on a precise
date to be fixed by mutual agreement between the Governments
of the countries entitled to take part in Consultative
Meetings.
