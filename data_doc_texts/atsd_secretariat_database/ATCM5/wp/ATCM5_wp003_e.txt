CHILEAN ADDITIONAL PROPOSAL
DRAFT AGENDA OF THE Vth. CONSULTATIVE MEETING
of the ANTARCTIC TREATY

Modifications to the agreed measures of Brussel's
Recommendation III-8 for preservations of living
resources in Antarctica

a) Cancelling of lettre d) of Article II as the disposition of
Art. III is considered as satisfactory :

"participating Governments will take appropriated measures
in order to ensure these agreed measures".

b) Modification of the writing of letter e) of Art.II
as follows :

" e) The "Authorization" formula means formal written licence
granted for the indicated purposes".

c) Modification of the writing of n° 6 of Art. VI as follows:
"The participating Governments will not allow granting of authori-
zation concerning an "especially protected specie", excepted in
accordance with the stipulations of paragraph 7 of this article".
