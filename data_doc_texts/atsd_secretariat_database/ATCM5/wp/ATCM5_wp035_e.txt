DRAFT RECOMMENDATION ARISING
FROM ITEM 8 OF THE AGENDA
(Commemorative Stamp Issue)

tabled by the French Delegation.

------------------------------------------------

The Representatives forward the following recommendations
to their Governments :

1)  that, in connection with the Tenth anniversary of the coming
into force of the Antarctic Treaty, each Member country
should issue a commemorative postage stamp during 1971 ;

2)  that the said stamp should bear, in the language or languages
of each issuing country, the words : Xth Anniversary of the
Antarctic Treaty, 1961 - 1971" ;

3)  that the most prominent design feature of the said stamp
should be constituted by the same representation of the
Antarctic Continent as appears on the official letter-
headings of the Antarctic Treaty Consultative Meetings,
with any additional matter to be consonant with the spirit
of the A[?]ctic Treaty covenants ;

4)  that the face-value of the said stamp, although remaining
at the discretion of the issuing country, should correspond
to an international rate of franking.
