ARGENTINE DELEGATION PROPOSAL
CONCERNING TELECOMMUNICATIONS
AND METEOROLOGICAL AFFAIRS MEETING

The Government of the Argentine Republic, confirming
the statement made by the Argentine Representative at the
Preparatory Meetings held on 24th September and 18th October 1968
in connection with the proposal to organise a meeting of
Government Telecommunications and Meteorological experts is
ready to provide facilities for such a meeting in Buenos Aires,
and suggests that a suitable date could be chosen during the
second half of 1969.
