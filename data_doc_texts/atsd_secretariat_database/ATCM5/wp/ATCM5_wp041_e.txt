DRAFT RECOMMENDATION
CONSIDERATION OF DRAFT CONVENTION FOR THE REGULATION
OF ANTARCTIC PELAGIC SEALING
(Proposed by the United States of America)

The Representatives, recognizing the need to consider the
drafting of a convention for the regulation of Antarctic pelagic
sealing,

Recommend to their Governments that they study the draft
Convention for the Regulation of Antarctic Pelagic Sealing appended
to this Recommendation before the next Consultative Meeting with
a view to the consideration of this draft Convention at that Meeting.
