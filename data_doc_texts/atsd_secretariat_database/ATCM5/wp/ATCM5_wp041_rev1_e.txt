DRAFT RECOMMENDATION

CONSIDERATION OF DRAFT CONVENTION FOR THE REGULATION
OF ANTARCTIC PELAGIC SEALING

(Submitted by the Sub-Working Group)

The Representatives, recognizing the need to consider
the drafting of a convention for the regulation of Antarctic
pelagic sealing,

Recommend to their Governments that before the next
Consultative Meeting, they study the draft Convention for the
Regulation of Antarctic Pelagic Sealing appended to the report
of the Fifth Consultative Meeting with a view to the considera-
tion of this draft Convention at the next Consultative Meeting.
