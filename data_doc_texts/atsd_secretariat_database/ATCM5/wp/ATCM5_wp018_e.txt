EXPLICATIVE NOTE PRESENTED BY THE UNION OF SOVIET
SOCIALIST REPUBLICS

Proposals on the creation of the Antarctic
Meteorological Regional Centers.

The wish of the creation of the antarctic meteoro-
logica Regional Centers, expressed by the U.S.S.R.,
Chile and the United States, has been evoked in OIM's
recommendation 6.CE-18.

The Regional Centers would be charged with:

1. To gather synoptic messages from the surface and
all aerological messages collected by the antarctic
stations, by the stations that are located on the islands
of the South Ocean, south of the 60th Parallel of South
and by the ships cruising South of the 60th Parallel of
the South.

2. Broadcast meteorological informations.

3. Carrying out research work.
