FACILITATION OF SCIENTIFIC RESEARCH: SITES
OF SPECIAL SCIENTIFIC INTEREST

(Recommendation to Plenary Submitted
by the Working Group on this Agenda Item)

The Representatives,

Recalling Recommendations VIII-3 and VIII-4;

Noting that in accordance with paragraph 2 of Recommendation VIII-3
SCAR, at its Fifteenth Meeting at Chamonix in May 1978, had reviewed
the seven sites of Special Scientific Interest designated in recom-
mendation VIII-4;

Noting that experience of the practical effect of the management
plans for these sites had shown them to be an effective means of
reducing the risk of harmful interference in areas of exceptional
scientific interest;

Noting that no change to these management plans had been proposed
by SCAR;

Recommend to their Governments that:

1.  The date of expiry of:

    Site No. 1:  Cape Royds, Ross Island

    Site No. 2:  Arrival Heights, Hut Point Peninsula, Ross Island

    Site No. 3:  Barwick Valley, Victoria Land

    Site No. 4:  Cape Crozier, Ross Island

    Site No. 5:  Fildes Peninsula, King George Island, South Shetland
Islands
ANT/X/31 (Rev. 1)                                  - 2 -

Site No. 6:  Byers Peninsula, Livingston Island, South Shetland 
             Islands

should be extended from 30 June 1981 to 30 June 1985.

2. The date of expiry of Site No. 7: Haswell Island, should be 
extended from 30 June 1981 to 30 June 1983.

3. They use their best endeavors to ensure, in accordance with 
paragraphs 3 and 4 of Recommendation VIII-3, that the manage-
ment plans of these sites are observed.
