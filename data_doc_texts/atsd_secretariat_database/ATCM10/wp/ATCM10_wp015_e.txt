PROPOSAL OF THE DELEGATION OF THE UNION OF SOVIET SOCIALIST REPUBLICS
CONCERNING THE PROCEDURES OF THE CONSULTATIVE MEETINGS ON THE ANTARCTIC TREATY

In accordance with Rule 25 of the Rules of Procedure of Consultative
Meetings, adopted on July 10, 1961, which provides for the possibility
of amending them, it is proposed that Rule 24 of the Rules read as
follows:

"The final report shall also contain a brief account of
the proceedings of the Meeting.  It will be approved by
all representatives present at the Meeting, and shall
be transmitted by the Secretary of the Meeting to all
Governments entitled to participate in the Meeting, for
their consideration."
