Draft Recommendation

Exchange of information
( Item 7 d )

(submitted by Germany)
Exchange of Information

The representatives

-   Recalling Recommendation VIII-6;
-   Taking into account experience made with the exchange of information according to Articles III and VII of the Antarctic Treaty;
-   Conscious of recent developments regarding the exchange of information between the operators of national Antarctic programs in the SCAR system;
Recommend to their Governments that:

    -   Paragraph 2: After the words "are to be furnished" the words "in general terms" are inserted.
    -   Paragraph 3, Subparagraph II, before the words "Dates of expeditions" the word "approximate" is inserted and before the words "duration of stay" the word "probable" is inserted;
    -   Paragraph 3, Subparagraph IV F, is substituted by the sentence "Personal engaged in scientific activities in general terms";
    -   Paragraph 3, Subparagraph VI: After the end of the Subparagraph the sentence "The detailed programs mentioned above are communicated between operators" is inserted;
    -   Paragraph 3, Subparagraph VI: After the word "work" the words "in general terms" are inserted;
    -   Paragraph 3, Subparagraph VIII, before the words "communication equipment" the word "principal" is inserted;
    -   Paragraph 3, Subparagraph VIII A is deleted;
    -   Paragraph 3, Subparagraph VIII 1. Sentence: the words "Transportation facilites" are deleted;
