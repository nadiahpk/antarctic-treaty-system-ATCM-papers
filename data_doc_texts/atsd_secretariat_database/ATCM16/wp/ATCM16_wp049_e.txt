Draft Recommendation

(submitted by Working Group II)
The Representatives,

Recalling Article VIII of the Agreed Measures for the Conservation of Antarctic Fauna and Flora and Recommendations V - 4, VI - 14, VII - 2, VII - 9, VIII - 3, [XV - 10, XV - 11]
Recalling the Protocol on Environmental Protection to the Antarctic Treaty done at Madrid on 4th October 1991;

Recommend to their Governments that:

1.      The Annex attached to this Recommendation form Annex V on Area Protection and Management to the Protocol on Environmental Protection to the Antarctic Treaty;

2.      The Annex become effective upon the date on which both the Protocol on Environmental Protection shall have entered into force and this Recommendation shall have been approved by all Consultative Parties entitled to attend the XVIth Antarctic Treaty Consultative Meeting;

3.      On the date on which the Annex becomes effective the following measures be terminated:
