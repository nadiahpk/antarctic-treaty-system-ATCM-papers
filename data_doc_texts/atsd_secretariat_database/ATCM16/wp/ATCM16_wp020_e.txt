DRAFT RECOMMENDATION

FACILITATION OF SCIENTIFIC RESEARCH:
SITES OF SPECIAL SCIENTIFIC INTEREST:  EXTENSION OF DESIGNATION

The Representatives,

Recalling Recommendations VIII-3, VIII-4, XII-5 and XIII-7;

Noting that:

    (i)     in accordance with paragraph 2 of Recommendation
            VIII-3, the Scientific Committee on Antarctic
            Research (SCAR), at its Twenty-First Meeting at
            Sao Paulo, Brazil (24-27 July, 1990) had reviewed
            the eight Sites of Special Scientific Interest
            designated in Recommendations VIII-4, XIII-8 and
            XIV-5;

    (ii)    experience of the practical effect of the Manage-
            ment Plans for these sites has shown them to be
            an effective means of reducing the risks of harm-
            ful interference in areas of special scientific
            interest;

   (iii)    no changes to the Management Plans of these sites
            had been proposed by SCAR.

Recommend to their Governments that:

1. The date of expiry of designation of Site Numbers:

        4 - Cape Crozier, Ross Island;

        5 - Fildes Peninsula, King George Island,
            South Shetland Islands;

MR2ABA/6
7 - Haswell Island;

10 - Caughley Beach, Cape Bird, Ross Island;

11 - Tramway Ridge, Mt Erebus, Ross Island;

12 - Canada Glacier, Lake Fryxell, Taylor Valley, 
Victoria Land;

18 - North-West White Island, McMurdo Sound

be extended to 31 December 2001.

2. The date of expiry of designation of Site Number:

22 - Yukidori Valley, Langhovde, Lutzow - Holm Bay

be extended to 31 December 2003.

3. They use their best endeavours to ensure, in 
accordance with paragraphs 3 and 4 of Recommendation 
VII-3, that the Management Plans of these sites are 
observed.
