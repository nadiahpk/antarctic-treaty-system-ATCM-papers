COMMEMORATION OF THE 30th ANNNIVERSARY OF THE ENTRY INTO FORCE
OF THE ANTARTCTIC TREATY

The Delegation of Chile hereby informs the Contracting Parties that the activities
organized by the country to celebrate the 30th anniversary of the entry into force of the
Antarctic Treaty included the issue of a postage stamp commemorating the event.

The cancellation ceremony for the postage stamp issue was held on 8 October 1991 at
Chile’s "Teniente Rodolfo Marsh" Antarctic Base, on King George Island.

The ceremony was presided over by the following authorities:

The Governor of the XIIth Region              : Mr. Roque Tomas Scarpa
The Chairman and Managing Director
of the Chilean Postal Agency              : Mr. Luis Minchel
The Manager of the Chilean Postal
Agency                                       : Mr. Mario Felmer

The Chief of Staff of the
IVth Air Command                            : Col. Florencio Duble

The Head of the INACH Planning
Department                                  : Mr. Daniel Torres

The event was widely reported in the country's media.
