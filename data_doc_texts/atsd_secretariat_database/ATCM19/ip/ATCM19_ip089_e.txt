ANTARCTIC INTERNATIONAL SCIENTIFIC COOPERATION

Peru offers cooperation in scientific and technological fields about the projects which are part of the 
National Plan of Scientific Research a the Antarctic for the middle term (1995-1999).

COOPERATION MODALITIES

1.  Join Research Projects

    To be carrying out between peruvian researches and researchers of other countries members 
    of the Antarctic Agreement through join scientific expeditions.

2.  Scientific Personnel Interchange

    a.  Participation of peruvian researchers in sciebtific expeditions of the others members 
        of the Antarctic Agreement and viceversa.

    b.  Research Trainning at the laboratories of the research institutes responsible of the 
        Antarctic Projects.

3.  International Meetings

    -   Workshops
    -   Congress
    -   Courses
    -   etc.

4.  Scientific Publication
