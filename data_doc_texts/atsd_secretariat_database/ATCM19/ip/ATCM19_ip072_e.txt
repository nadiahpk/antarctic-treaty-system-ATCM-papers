ACCESSING U.S. IEEs ON THE WORLD WIDE WEB

Agenda Item 11 a

(Submitted by the United States of America)
US IEEs are available on the World Wide Web (WWW) from the 
National Science Foundation (NSF) Office of Polar Programs (OPP).

To access information on NSF and NSF/OPP, point your WWW 
browser to the following URLs:

NSF homepage: http://www.nsf.gov/

NSF/OPP homepage: http://x.nsf.gov:80/opp/antarct/antarct.htm

For more information on NSF/OPP: 
http://icair.iac.org.nz/reports/us/index.html

For information on how to get connected to the WWW, and how to 
get the necessary software, please contact the Institute for Global 
Communications: Ph.+1-415-442-0220,  Fax+1-415-546-1794, 
email: support@igc.org
