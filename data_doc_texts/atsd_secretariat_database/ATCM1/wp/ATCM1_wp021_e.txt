AGENDA ITEM 16

ARRANGEMENTS FOR RADIO COMMUNICATIONS

Submitted by : the Argentine Delegation

The Representatives recommend:

(1)      that Governments should convene a meeting of communications
         specialists to study the telecommunication facilities needed
         for scientific and technical purposes to and within the Treaty
         Area;

(2)      that the Contracting Parties entitled to participate in
         Meetings under Article IX of the Antarctic Treaty, the United
         Nations Specialized agencies and other international organizations
         having a scientific or technical interest in the zone of application
         of the Treaty should be invited to inform the Meeting of their
         scientific and technical requirements for telecommunications to
         and within Antarctica.
