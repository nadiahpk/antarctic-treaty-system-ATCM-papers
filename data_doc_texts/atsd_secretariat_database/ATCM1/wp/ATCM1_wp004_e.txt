AGENDA ITEM 8

EXCHANGE AND MAKING AVAILABLE OF SCIENTIFIC
OBSERVATIONS AND RESULTS

The following recommendation circulated at the request of the
United States Delegation was adopted:

"The representatives recommend to their Governments that they
should promote the exchange and making available of observations and results
from Antarctica through the recognised international data gathering centers
and by such other means as may be appropriate to ensure the exchange and
free availability of this information."
