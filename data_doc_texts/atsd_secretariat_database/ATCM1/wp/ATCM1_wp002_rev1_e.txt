AGENDA ITEM NO. 7:

EXCHANGE OF SCIENTIFIC PERSONNEL

Submitted by : New Zealand

A recommendation circulated at the request of the New
Zealand Delegation was amended and adopted in the following form :-

The Representatives recomend to their Governments
that they should promote the continuation of the
exchange, on a basis of bilateral arrangements, of
scientific personnel amongst their expeditions, and
should make available such of their facilities as
may be helpful to this purpose.
