TO AGENDA ITEM 16

MEASURES ON RADIO COMMUNICATION

Submitted by the Delegation of the Union of
Soviet Socialist Republics

Representatives recomend to their Governments to
assist the activities of SCAR in coordinating and improving
radio communication in the Antarctica and in particular
urges SCAR Working Group of Communications to study and
develop proposals on following matters:

1. The most appropriate system of routes
required by users of radio communications
2. Methods of transmission
3. Necessary power of effective reception
