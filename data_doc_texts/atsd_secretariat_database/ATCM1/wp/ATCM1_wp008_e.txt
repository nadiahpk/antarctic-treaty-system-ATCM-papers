AGENDA ITEM 15

RECIPROCAL ASSISTANCE AMONG EXPEDITIONS

France but subsequently amended, was adopted on 13th July 1961:-

The Representatives reaffirm the traditional
Antarctic principle that expeditions render all assistance
feasible in the event of an emergency request for help and
recommend to their Governments that consideration should
be given to arranging consultations among them, and to the
matter being discussed at the appropriate time at any
meeting of experts qualified to discuss it.
