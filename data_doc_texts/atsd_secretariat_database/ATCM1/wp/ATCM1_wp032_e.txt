Agenda Item No. 18

Application of Nuclear Energy in the Treaty Area

Submitted by South Africa

"In proposing this item the South African Government has in mind
the desirability of exploring the means whereby all participating states
would be able to keep abreast of those developments in the field of the
safe and peaceful uses of nuclear energy which are of particular application
to Antarctica or which would be of particular value in promoting Antarctic
research and exploration."
