AGENDA ITEM 19                QUESTIONS OF PROCEDURE

Proposal by the Chilean Delecation

The Representatives recomend to their Governments that the
distribution and circulation of documentation in connection with the
Consultative Meetings provided for in Article IX of the Treaty, as also
the exchange of information regarding processing by each Government of
the Recomendations approved by the Representatives and the said Meetings,
should follow the procedure set forth below:

1. As soon as a Meeting is ended the Government of the host country
shall send to each of the other participating Governments a
registered copy of the Final Report containing the authentic
texts of all documents agreed and adopted by the Meeting. It
shall undertake, furthermore to send to the other participating
Governments any other documents relative to the Meeting, comply
with any additional request or answer any questions on the
subject, and to supply any information which the participating
Governments may request subsequently regarding the Meeting held
in its territory.

2. Notification of approval or rejection by the participating
Governments of the Recomendations made at the Consultative
Meeting shall be sent to each of the other Parties through
normal diplomatic channels.

3. The preparation of the succeeding Consultative Meeting shall be
the responsiblity of the Government of the country where it is
to be held, and the latter shall consult the other Governments
entitled to participate in the Consultative Meeting, either
