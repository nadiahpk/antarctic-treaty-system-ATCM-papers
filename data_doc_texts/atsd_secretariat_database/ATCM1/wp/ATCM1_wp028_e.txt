ADMINISTRATIVE ARRANGEMENTS
Submitted by: United Kingdom

The Representatives recommend to their Governments that,
without prejudice to the position of any Government about future
arrangements, all essential services, including the receipt of
notification of acceptance by Governments of recommendations accepted
by the present meeting, should for the time being be entrusted to the
Government of Australia.
