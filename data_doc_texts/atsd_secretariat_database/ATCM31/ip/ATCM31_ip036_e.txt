Annual Report pursuant to the Protocol on Environmental Protection to the Antarctic Treaty 

This report has been prepared in compliance with article 17 of the Protocol on Environmental Protection to the Antarctic Treaty. It should be noted that some Belgian Antarctic activities during the period 2007-2008 were embedded in other national or international expeditions, thereby using the facilities and logistics of other nations. 


    1. NOTIFICATION PURSUANT TO ARTICLE 17 OF THE PROTOCOL ON ENVIRONMENTAL PROTECTION TO THE ANTARCTIC TREATY

        1.1 Notification of the measures taken pursuant to Article 13, paragraphs 1 and 2 of the Protocol

No compliance measures have been taken. 

        1.2 Contingency plans established in accordance with Article 15 of the Protocol

No contingency plans were set up

    2. REPORT PURSUANT TO ANNEX I, ARTICLE 6 (1) OF THE ENVIRONMENTAL PROTOCOL

        2.1 Procedures to consider the environmental impacts of proposed activities in Antarctica / Annex I, article 6 (1a)

All activities in Antarctica undertaken were in compliance with the requirements of the regulations on the terms and procedures of implementing the Protocol on environmental protection.

        2.2 List of Initial Environmental Evaluations developed in accordance with Article 2 and decisions taken / Annex I, Article 6 (1b)

During the period 2007-2008 no Initial Environmental Evaluations have been prepared. 

        2.3 Significant information obtained, and any action taken in consequence thereof, from procedures put in place in accordance with Annex I, Articles 2 (2) and 5, of the Protocol (Annex I, Article 6 (1c))

During the period 2007-2008 no significant information has been obtained in accordance with the provisions of Annex I.

    3. REPORT PURSUANT TO ARTICLE 6 OF ANNEX II TO THE ENVIRONMENTAL PROTOCO

        3.1 Number and nature of permits issued under art. 6/2 of Annex II to the Protocol during the 2007-2008 season

During the period 2007-2008, two permits have been issued under Annex II of the Protocol. The first one is related to the BELARE 2007-2008 expedition (November 2007 - March 2008) for the construction of the Belgian Research Station at Utsteinen. The second one is related to the non-governmental expedition In the Wake of the Belgica (sailing boat Euronav Belgica) in the Antarctic peninsula (December 2007 - February 2008).

    4. REPORT PURSUANT TO ARTICLE  9 OF ANNEX III TO THE ENVIRONMENTAL PROTOCOL

        4.1 Waste Management Plans prepared in accordance with Article 8 of Annex III to the Protocol / Article 9/1 of Annex III

The overall waste management strategy of the Belgian Research Program on Antarctica is that all waste, with the exception of grey water, and some human waste generated in field camps, is retrograded out of the Antarctic Treaty Area for proper disposal.

        4.2 Report on implementation of waste management plan/ Article 9/1 of Annex III to the Protocol

All waste, with the exception of grey water, is carried away from the Antarctic Treaty Area for proper disposal.

        4.3 Inventory of past activities in accordance with Article 8/3 of Annex III to the Protocol / Article 9/1 

No inventory of passed activities has been drawn up at present time.
