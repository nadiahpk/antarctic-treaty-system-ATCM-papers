Accession of Turkey to the Protocol on Environmental Protection to the Antarctic Treaty 

Summary

This paper gives a brief report on Turkey’s accession to the Protocol on Environmental Protection to the Antarctic Treaty and the way forward.

Introduction

The law on ratification of the Protocol on Environmental Protection to the Antarctic Treaty and all of its Annexes has been published in 24.05.2017 dated and 30075 numbered official gazette. Following this, standard procedures have been employed and Depositary Government has been notified. 

As stated in the Circular 30/2017 published by Secretariat on 07/12/2017, Depositary Government has informed Secretariat that Turkey has acceded to the Protocol on Environmental Protection to the Antarctic Treaty. The official date of the deposit of the instrument of accession was 27 September 2017.

In addition to this information, Turkey has been working on transposing Protocol on Environmental Protection to the Antarctic Treaty to its domestic law. Other countries’ examples are evaluated, the legislations that are foreseen to be amended accordingly are also examined. Moreover, stakeholder institutions are determined, and a coordination meeting is foreseen among relevant institutions.
