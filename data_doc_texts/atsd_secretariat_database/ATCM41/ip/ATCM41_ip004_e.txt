COMNAP Search and Rescue (SAR) Workshop IV
Advance announcement of details
Information Paper submitted by the Council of Managers of National Antarctic Programs (COMNAP)

Noting ATCM XXXVI Resolution 4 (2013) Improved Collaboration on Search and Rescue (SAR) in Antarctica, in particular, recommendation 7a, which recommends that the Parties: 

“support COMNAP to continue to foster collaborative discussions and vital sharing of information regarding SAR matters including through: holding triennial workshops on search and rescue, that include representatives of the RCCs, National Antarctic Programs, relevant experts, private operators as well as commercial emergency notification service providers, and inform future ATCMs on the results of these workshops;”.

COMNAP wishes to advise the ATCM that the next COMNAP SAR Workshop, which will be Workshop IV, will take place in New Zealand from 14­–17 May 2019.  The workshop will be co-hosted by Rescue Coordination Centre New Zealand (RCCNZ), Maritime New Zealand (based in Wellington) and COMNAP Member National Antarctic Program Antarctica New Zealand (based in Christchurch).

The Workshop will be convened in Wellington and then Christchurch.
The dates would be:	
Day 1 (in Wellington) – Tuesday 14 May 2019
Day 2 (travel day) – Wednesday 15 May 2019
Day 3 and Day 4 (both days in Christchurch) – 16 & 17 May 2019

As per Resolution 4 (2013) “…representatives of the RCCs, National Antarctic Programs, relevant experts, private operators as well as commercial emergency notification service providers” will be encouraged to participate. 

COMNAP hopes Parties will support participation of their relevant delegates to attend this workshop and requests Parties to communicate widely the confirmed date for COMNAP SAR Workshop IV for advance planning purposes.
