ANT/INF.12
31 Octoter 1970

CLOSING SPELCH BY AMBASSADOR A. MATSUI,
THE CHAIRMAN OF THE SIXTH CONSULTATIVE MEETING, ON 31 OCTOBER 1970

I would like to express my deepest gratitude to all of those who
have honoured me with kind words. I would also like to express my sincere
thanks to all of you for the spirit of cooperation with which you have
undertaken the deliberations in this Sixth Antarctic Treaty Consultative
Meeting.

    I sincerely hope that this spirit of cooperation, as well as the
agreements which have been made in this Meeting, will contribute further
to our future activities in the Antarctic.
