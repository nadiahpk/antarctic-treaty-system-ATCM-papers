ANT/INF.13
31 October 1970

CLOSING STATEMENT BY PROFESSOR T. NAGATA,
REPRESENTATIVE OF JAPAN, ON 31 OCTOBER 1970

Mr. Chairman,

On behalf of the Japanese Delegation, I would like to express my sincere gratitude to all the other Delegations for their kind remarks which they have made in referring to my country. I would also like to express my respect to the members of the Secretariat for their efficient services and arrangements which they have rendered for this Meeting and its Working Groups.

Mr. Chairman, I would just like to add that as the host the Japanese Government will, in accordance with the Recommendation I-XIV, send a certified copy of the Final Report of this Meeting to each Government which has participated in this Meeting.

Thank you,
