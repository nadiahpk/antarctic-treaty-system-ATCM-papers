Draft Recommendation submitted by United Kingdom

HISTORIC MONUMENTS

(The United Kingdom submits the following
provisional draft Recommendation relating to Historic
monuments as an indication of one way in which this
item could be handled:)

The Representatives, re-affirming Recommendation
I-IX and recalling Recommendation V-4, recommend to
their Governments:

(1)    that they adopt all adequate measures to
preserve and protect from damage the historic
monuments situated in the Antarctic Treaty
Area and described in the list annexed to
this Recommendation, thereby ensuring the
survival of such historic monuments;

(2)    that they arrange for each of these historical
monuments to be appropriately marked with a
notice indicating in the English, French,
Russian and Spanish languages that it is
scheduled for preservation in accordance with
the provisions of the Antarctic Treaty.
