ANTARCTIC METEOROLOGY
(WMO Document No.17.823/T/AA/TR, July 23, 1969)

Taking into consideration requirements determined
in the Second Session of the Working Group on the
Antarctic Meteorology and stated in the letter to the
Member States from the Secretary-General of the World
Meteorological Organization No.17.823/T/AA/TR dated
July 23, 1969, of meteorological information (processed
and not processed) of the Member States,

Considering intentions of some Member States to
create in Antarctica the meteorological centers having
functions of collection, treatment and transmission of
meteorological data;

The Representatives recommend to their governments
that;

1)     they take into consideration requirements of
meteorological information of the Member States
and the list of the centers expected to receive
necessary processed informations,

2)     as far as possible, they provide for taking
necessary measures to their Antarctic centers and

stations
- 2 -

stations in order to satisfy the requirements,
taking account of well-timed collection (including
reports from expedition, ship and plane), treatment
(at data processing centers) and transmission of
necessary meteorological information.
