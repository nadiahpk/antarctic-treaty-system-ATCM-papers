DRAFT RECOMMENDATION SUBMITTED BY THE UNITED STATES

EXCHANGE OF INFORMATION ON

OCEAN RESEARCH VESSELS

The Representatives,

Noting the increased activities of ocean research vessels conducting
scientific programs in the Antarctic Treaty area:

Noting further that Article VII(5) of the Antarctic Treaty includes
provision for exchange of information about ocean research vessels:

Recommend to their governments that they exchange not later than the
end of November of each year as part of the annual exchange of informa-
tion carried out under terms of Article VII(5) of the Treaty the follow-
ing information concerning the activities of ocean research vessels in
the Treaty Area:

a) Name of the vessels, and the officers in charge of each vessel;

b) Scientific programs of all cruises in the Treaty Area, together
with the names of the scientists participating therein;

c) Geographical coordinates of the proposed area of operation of
such vessels including tracks and schedules if available and the
communications information required in order to make contact with them.
