REVIEW OF PROGRESS IN IMPLEMENTING THE AGREED
MEASURES FOR THE CONSERVATION OF ANTARCTIC FAUNA AND FLORA

(Submitted by the United Kingdom)

The United Kingdom submits the two attached tables for discussion
under this item of the Agenda. The United Kingdom Representative will
suggest the need for "nil" returns to be supplied annually in cases
where no animals have been killed or captured.
