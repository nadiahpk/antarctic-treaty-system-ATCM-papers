Draft Recommendation on

Historic Monuments

(As amended by the Working Group on item 9 on 22 October 1970.)

The Representatives, taking into account Recommendations I-IX
and V-4, recommend to their Governments:

(1) that they undertake such measures as are feasible to ensure
    the survival of the historic monuments situated in the
    Antarctic Treaty Area and described in the list annexed to this
    Recommendation, to which agreed modifications may be made
    from time to time;

(2) that they arrange, when practicable, for each of these
    historic monuments to be appropriately marked with a notice
    indicating in the English, French, Russian and Spanish
    languages that it is scheduled for preservation in accordance
    with the provisions of the Antarctic Treaty.
