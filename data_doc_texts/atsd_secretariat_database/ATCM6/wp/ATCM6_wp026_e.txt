DRAFT RECOMMENDATION

SUBMITTED BY AUSTRALIA AND THE UNITED KINGDOM

CONSERVATION DATA

The Representatives,

Recalling Recommendations III-X and IV-19;

Noting that, in accordance with the interim guidelines contained in
Recommendation IV-20, information is already being exchanged;

Noting further that this information may be freely published and that
it is desirable that it should be amalgamated in a form most useful
for scientific analysis;

Recommend to their governments that:

1. they transmit the information exchanged under Recommendation
IV-19, to their National Antarctic Committees;

2. that, through those National Committees, they invite the
Scientific Committee on Antarctic Research (SCAR) to assemble
the information exchanged under Article XII of the Agreed
Measures for the Conservation of Antarctic Fauna and Flora
and, in accordance with Recommendation IV-19, to prepare
reports from time to time on the status of species.
