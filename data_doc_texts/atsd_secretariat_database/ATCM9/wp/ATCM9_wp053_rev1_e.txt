DRAFT TERMS OF REFERENCE FOR WORKING GROUP ON LEGAL/POLITICAL
ASPECTS OF ANTARCTIC MINERAL RESOURCES
(Submitted by the U.S. Delegation)

A Working Group shall be established to examine some
legal, political and other related aspects of the question
of Antarctic Mineral Resources, to report on its work and
to propose a possible draft recommendation.
