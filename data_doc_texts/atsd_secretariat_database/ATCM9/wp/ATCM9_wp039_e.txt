TERMS OF REFERENCE FOR WORKING GROUP ON MARINE LIVING RESOURCES

Text of proposal by the Delegation of New Zealand approved
at Heads of Delegation meeting on 21 September 1977:

A Working Group shall be established to examine the
question of Marine Living Resources in all its aspects,
scientific, legal, political, or other including:

    (a) Possible interim measures;

    (b) Measures for the further development of
    a marine living resources regime.

The Working Group shall consider all papers and
proposals put before it, report, and make draft recommendations.
