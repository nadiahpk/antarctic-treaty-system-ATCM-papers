Agenda Item 6              

MARINE LIVING RESOURCES - WORKING GROUP              

1. The Plenary Session on 23 September 1977 approved the draft              
Terms of Reference for the Working Group on Marine Living Resources              
given in ANT/IX/39. Acceptance of the Terms of Reference was              
proposed by the United Kingdom delegation and seconded by the              
Australian delegation.              

2. In Committee the delegation of Australia proposed, and the              
delegation of the Soviet Union seconded, the appointment of              
Ambassador J.G. McArthur (New Zealand) as Chairman of the Working              
Group. This proposal was agreed.
