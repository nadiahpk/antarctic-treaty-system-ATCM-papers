EXPERT WORKING GROUP ON MINERALS
NOTE FOR THE RECORD

At its session on 21 September The Group agreed the Agenda
prepared by the Chairman (paper ANT/1X/27) subject to the
correction of linguistic points on the Russian text and to
the following amendment proposed by The Delegate of
Argentina:

    Amend item 4 to read

    "To suggest preliminary guidelines on appropriate
methods for exploration and exploitation and
on preventive, corrective and restorative measures
for the protection of the environment."
