Agenda Item 8

EFFECTS OF TOURISTS AND NON-GOVERNMENTAL EXPEDITIONS

1. At the Plenary Session on 23 September 1977 it was agreed that an informal Working Group should be established to consider aspects of tourism in the Antarctic.

2. The Group, under the direction of the Delegation of Chile, would be composed of Representatives of Argentina, Belgium, Poland, the Soviet Union and the United Kingdom.
