Report of the Depositary Government for the Agreement on the Conservation of Albatrosses and Petrels (ACAP)
Information Paper submitted by Australia
Abstract
A report is provided by Australia as Depositary of the Agreement on the Conservation of Albatrosses and Petrels 2001.
Background	
Australia, as Depositary of the Agreement on the Conservation of Albatrosses and Petrels 2001 (Agreement) is pleased to report to the Thirty-fifth Antarctic Treaty Consultative Meeting on the status of the Agreement.
Australia advises the Antarctic Treaty Parties that, since the Thirty-fourth Antarctic Treaty Consultative Meeting, no States have acceded to the Agreement. 
A copy of the status list for the Agreement is available via the internet on the Australian Treaties Database at the following address: 
http://www.austlii.edu.au/au/other/dfat/treaty_list/depository/consalbnpet.html
The status list is also available upon request to the Treaties Secretariat of the Australian Government Department of Foreign Affairs and Trade.  Requests can be conveyed through Australian diplomatic missions.
