IMPORTATION OF LABORATORY ANIMALS AND PLANTS
(Draft Recommendation submitted by Australia, New Zealand,
South Africa, the United Kingdom and the United States)

The Representatives,

Considering:

(1) that harmful interference with the natural
ecological system may be caused by micro-organisms
introduced by man for experimental purposes;

(2) Article IX of the Agreed Measures for the
Conservation of Antarctic Fauna and Flora;

Recommend to their Governments that the following be
added to the end of paragraph (c) of Annex C, Importation
of Animals and Plants, of the Agreed Measures for the
Conservation of Antarctic Fauna and Flora:

"including viruses, bacteria, yeasts and fungi".
