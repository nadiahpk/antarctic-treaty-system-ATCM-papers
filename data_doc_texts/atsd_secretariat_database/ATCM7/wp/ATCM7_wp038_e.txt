ADMINISTRATIVE ARRANGEMENTS
(Draft of Recommendation submitted by Chile)

The Representatives,

Recommend to their Governments that Recommendation I-XIV
be supplemented by the following point:

"At the conclusion of the Consultative Meeting, the
host country will send a complete set of all documents
circulated thereat, as well as the Final Report, to the
host country of the next Consultative Meeting".
