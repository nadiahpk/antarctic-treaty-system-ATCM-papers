Report of the Joint Inspections’ Program undertaken by Argentina and Chile under Article VII of the Antarctic Treaty and Article 14 of the Environmental Protocol
Information paper submitted by Argentina and Chile
Summary
Between February 16 and 18th, 2016, a Chilean-Argentine joint team of observers conducted inspection activities to Antarctic facilities in the South Shetland Islands’ region, according to what is established by the Antarctic Treaty’s Article VII and the Environmental Protocol’s Article 14. The document attached to this Information Paper is the full report of such an Inspection Program. This Information paper complements Working Paper 44 (General Recommendations from the Joint Inspections Undertaken by Argentina and Chile under Article VII of the Antarctic Treaty and Article 14 of the Environmental Protocol) submitted by Argentina and Chile to the XXXIX ATCM.
