REPORT OF THE UNITED STATES OF AMERICA 
WITH RESPECT TO ARTICLE 13(1) OF THE PROTOCOL ON 
ENVIRONMENTAL PROTECTION TO THE ANTARCTIC TREATY 
 
 
 
 
The United States ratified the Protocol on 
Environmental Protection to the Antarctic Treaty, which 
entered into force on 14 January 1998, on 17 April 1997.  
In conformity with Article 13(1) of the Protocol, 
existing U.S. implementing legislation and regulations 
are annexed to this paper.
