Annual Report Pursuant to the Protocol on

Protection to the Antarctic Treaty 
 
 
 
 
 
This report has been prepared in compliance with article 17 of the Protocol on Environmental Protection to the 
Antarctic Treaty. 
 
 
1. NOTIFICATION PURSUANT TO ARTICLE 17 OF THE PROTOCOL ON ENVIRONMENTAL 
PROTECTION TO THE ANTARCTIC TREATY 
 
1.1 Notification of the measures taken pursuant to Article 13, paragraphs 1 and 2 of the Protocol 
 
 
The Madrid Protocol has been ratified by the Belgian regional parliaments in 1994/1995. The federal parliament 
has approved this ratification by its law of May 19, 1995. 
 
The legislative act to execute this law has been examined by the Council of State in January 2001, and sent back 
to parliament. It is expected that this executive act will be ratified by the federal parliament in the very near 
future. 
 
In what follows, it should be realized that Belgian Antarctic activities during the period 2000-2001 were 
embedded in other national or international expeditions, thereby using the facilities and logistics of other  
nations.  
 
 
1.2 Contingency plans established in accordance with Article 15 of the Protocol 
 
No contingency plans were set up. 
 
 
 
2. REPORT PURSUANT TO ANNEX I, ARTICLE 6 (1) OF THE ENVIRONMENTAL PROTOCOL 
 
2.1 Procedures to consider the environmental impacts of proposed activities in Antarctica / Annex I, 
article 6 (1a) 
 
 
 
All activities in Antarctica undertaken were in compliance with the requirements of the regulations on the 
terms and procedures of implementing the protocol on environmental protection. 
 
2.2 List of Initial Environmental Evaluations developed in accordance with Article 2 and decisions 
taken/Annex I, article 6 (1b) 
 
During the period 2000-2001 no initial environmental evaluations have been prepared. 
 
2.3 Significant information obtained, and any action taken in consequence thereof, from procedures put 
in place in accordance with Annex 1, Articles 2(2) and 5, of the Protocol (Annex I, Article 6 (1c)) 
 
During the period 2000-2001 no significant information has been obtained in accordance with the 
provisions of Annex I. 
 
 
3. REPORT PURSUANT TO ANNEX II, ARTICLE 6 OF THE ENVIRONMENTAL PROTOCOL 
 
3.1 Number and nature of permits issued under Annex II of the Protocol during the period 1 September 
2000 to 1July 2001/ Annex II, article 6/2 of the Protocol. 
 
 
During the period 2000-2001 no permits have been issued under Annex II. 
 
 
4. REPORT PURSUANT TO ANNEX III, ARTICLE 9 OF THE ENVIRONMENTAL PROTOCOL 
 
 
4.1 Waste Management Plans prepared in accordance with Annex III, article 8 of the Protocol / Annex 
III, article 9/1 of the Protocol 
 
The overall waste management strategy of the Belgian Research Program on Antarctica is that all waste, with 
the exception of grey water, and some human waste generated in field camps, is retrograded out of the Antarctic 
Treaty Area for proper disposal. 
 
 
4.2 Report on implementation of waste management plan / Annex III, article 9/1 of the Protocol 
 
All waste, with the exception of grey water is carried away from the Antarctic  
Treaty Area for proper disposal. 
 
 
4.3 Inventory of past activities in accordance with Annex III, article 8/3 of the Protocol / Annex III, 
article 9/1 of the Protocol 
 
No inventory of passed activities has been drawn up at present time.
