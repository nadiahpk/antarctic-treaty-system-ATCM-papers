ANNUAL REPORT PURSUANT TO THE PROTOCOL ON ENVIRONMENTAL 
PROTECTION ON THE ANTARCTIC TREATY 
 
 
FINLAND (SEASON 2000-2001) 
 
 
 
1. NOTIFICATION PURSUANT TO ARTICLE 17 OF THE PROTOCOL ON 
ENVIRONMENTAL PROTECTION TO THE ANTARCTIC TREATY 
 
1.1 Notification of the Measures taken pursuant to Article 13, paragraphs 1 and 2 of the Protocol 
 
Finland has taken the following measures to ensure compliance with the Protocol on Environmental 
Protection to the Antarctic Treaty: 
 
- Adoption of the Act on the Environment Protection of Antarctica of 18 October 1996 (Finnish 
legislation serial number 28/1998); 
 
- Adoption of the Act on Amendment of the Penal Code (Finnish legislation serial number 29/1998); 
 
- Adoption of the Decree on the Environmental Protection of Antarctica of 13 February 1998 (Finnish 
legislation serial number 122/1998). 
 
 
The Act on the Environmental Protection of Antarctica and the Act on Amendment of the Penal Code 
entered into force on 14 January 1998 while the Decree on the Environmental Protection of Antarctica 
entered into force on 23 February 1998. Several questionnaires and guidelines have been developed as 
common procedures for Norway, Sweden and Finland. 
 
 
1.2 Contingency plans established in accordance with Article 15 of the Protocol 
 
An Oil Spill Contingency Plan for the Finnish research station Aboa was finalised in 1999. The 
framework for the plan has been developed in co-operation with the Norwegian and Swedish Antarctic 
Programmes.

2.2 List of Initial Environmental Evaluations (IEE) developed in accordance with Article 2 and

Research expedition to Dronning Maud Land 
Responsible: Finnish Institute of Marine Research 
Decision:

planned.

Article 6(1d))
