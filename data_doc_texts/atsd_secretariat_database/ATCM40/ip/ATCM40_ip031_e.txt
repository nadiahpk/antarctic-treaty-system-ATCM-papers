Report of the Depositary Government for the Agreement on the Conservation of Albatrosses and Petrels (ACAP)
Information paper submitted by Australia
Abstract

A report is provided by Australia as Depositary of the Agreement on the Conservation of Albatrosses and Petrels 2001.
Background

Australia, as Depositary of the Agreement on the Conservation of Albatrosses and Petrels 2001 (‘the Agreement’) is pleased to report to the Fortieth Antarctic Treaty Consultative Meeting (ATCM XL) on the status of the Agreement.

Australia advises Antarctic Treaty Parties that, since the Thirty-Ninth Antarctic Treaty Consultative Meeting (ATCM XXXIX), no States have acceded to the Agreement.

A copy of the status list for the Agreement is available, via the internet, on the Australian Treaties Database at the following address:

http://www.austlii.edu.au/au/other/dfat/treaty_list/depository/consalbnpet.html 

The status list is also available on request to the Treaties Secretariat of the Australian Government Department of Foreign Affairs and Trade.  Requests can be conveyed through Australian diplomatic missions.
