Ratification of Protocol on Environmental Protection to the Antarctic Treaty by Turkey

The Grand National Assembly of Turkey has ratified the Protocol on Environmental Protection to the Antarctic Treaty at the plenary session on 14.02.2017. 

The law on ratification of the Protocol on Environmental Protection to the Antarctic Treaty has been published in 08.03.2017 dated and 30001 numbered official gazette.
